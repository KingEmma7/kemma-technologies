## Overview

Constract is a multi-vendor marketplace connecting buyers with vendors of building materials and construction products. Kemma Technologies designed and built the platform end-to-end — from product catalog to checkout to vendor operations.

## The Challenge

Bringing multiple vendors onto a single marketplace requires more than a storefront. It needs distinct, secure experiences for buyers, vendors and administrators, plus reliable order tracking from checkout through to delivery.

## Our Approach

We built Constract on Next.js with Supabase handling authentication and data, implementing role-based access across three distinct portals: a customer-facing storefront with cart and checkout, a vendor dashboard for managing listings and orders, and a super-admin console for platform oversight. Order tracking, profile management and a materials and product catalog service round out the core platform.

## Outcomes

- Live, in-production marketplace processing real customer orders
- Distinct authenticated experiences for customers, vendors and admins
- Full order lifecycle from cart to tracking, with no manual intervention required
