## Overview

Estee's Bakery wanted a website that matched the warmth of its brand while making it easy for customers to browse the menu and place custom cake orders.

## The Challenge

Bakery orders are often custom — size, flavor, occasion — and that complexity needed to feel simple and inviting online, not like filling out a form.

## Our Approach

We designed a Next.js site with a soft, custom pastel palette, using Framer Motion and GSAP for playful scroll and hover animation. A two-step order flow captures cake specifications clearly, backed by a menu page and testimonials section.

## Outcomes

- Live at esteesbakery.com
- Actively used by customers to place real orders
- Custom order flow reduces back-and-forth between customer and bakery
