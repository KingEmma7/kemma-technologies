## Overview

Kofi Asiedu Mahama needed a home for his books online — a fast, polished landing page where readers could learn about his work and buy directly, without relying on third-party retailers alone.

## The Challenge

Selling digital books directly means handling payments, delivering files securely, and building trust with readers, all while keeping the experience simple and fast on any device.

## Our Approach

We built the site on Next.js with Paystack for secure checkout, time-limited signed URLs for digital downloads, and Supabase for backend data. A newsletter integration keeps readers engaged after purchase, and the design is optimized for performance and mobile.

## Outcomes

- Live at kofiasiedumahama.com
- Approximately 100 books sold directly through the platform
- Secure, self-serve purchase and download flow with no manual fulfillment
