## Overview

RetailEdge operates one of West Africa's fastest-growing e-commerce platforms. They engaged Kemma Technologies to design and deploy a personalization engine that would surface the right product to the right customer at the right moment.

## The Challenge

Generic product listings were generating low click-through rates. Without personalization, customers were leaving without discovering products relevant to them, and average order value was plateauing.

## Our Approach

We built a collaborative filtering model trained on 18 months of anonymized purchase and browsing history. The model outputs are served through a low-latency REST API, with a Redis cache layer ensuring sub-50ms response times at scale. A/B testing infrastructure was embedded from day one so the team could iterate the model continuously.

## Outcomes

- **&#43;34%** conversion rate uplift (holdout A/B test)
- **&#43;22%** average order value
- **&lt;40ms** median recommendation API latency
- Fully GDPR/NDPA compliant data handling
