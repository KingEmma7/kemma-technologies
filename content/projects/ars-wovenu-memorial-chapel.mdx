## Overview

The Apostles Revelation Society – Wovenu Memorial Chapel needed a modern web presence to connect with its congregation and the wider community online.

## The Challenge

Many church websites feel dated or hard to navigate. The chapel wanted a site that felt current and trustworthy, while remaining simple to browse for members of all ages.

## Our Approach

We built a responsive Next.js site with Tailwind CSS and Framer Motion, focused on clean information architecture, accessibility and fast load times across devices.

## Outcomes

- Live at arswovenumemorialchapel.org
- In active use by the church and its congregation
