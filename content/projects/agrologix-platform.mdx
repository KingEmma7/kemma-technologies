## Overview

AgroLogix is a mobile-first web platform designed to bridge the gap between Ghanaian smallholder farmers and the digital economy. It provides real-time commodity pricing, crop health diagnostics powered by computer vision, and direct access to micro-financing products.

## The Challenge

Over 2 million smallholder farmers in Ghana operate without access to financial services or reliable market information. AgroLogic Africa approached Kemma Technologies to design and build an accessible platform that could work on low-bandwidth connections while delivering enterprise-grade functionality.

## Our Approach

We adopted a progressive web app (PWA) architecture — installable, offline-capable and fast on 2G/3G connections. The AI diagnostics module was built with TensorFlow.js, allowing crop disease detection directly in the browser without a server round-trip. For the financial module, we integrated with mobile money APIs prevalent across the region.

## Outcomes

- **2.3x** increase in market access for enrolled farmers
- **34%** reduction in post-harvest losses through early disease detection
- **18,000+** active monthly users within 6 months of launch
- Featured in the Ghana Innovation Awards 2024
